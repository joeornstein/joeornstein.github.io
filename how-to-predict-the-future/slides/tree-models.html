Information Theory: Review

Recall the definitions of information and entropy.

  • Information is how surprised we are when we learn the outcome. \(-log(p)\).

  • Entropy is “average surprise”: how much surprise we expect to experience when we learn the outcome.

  • If we could perfectly predict the Rotten Tomatoes rating a of movie, then entropy would be zero. (We would never be surprised).

  • Optimal strategy is to seek out information that reduces entropy the most.
